How to Use the Expense Tracker

The Expense Tracker is designed to help you keep track of your monthly expenses, giving you an overview of the totals by category and show you were most of your money goes!

  1. You can reset all the categories to $0 simply by clicking on the delete/reset button
  2. As you add your expenses the tracker will add them to your existing ones.
  3. If you want you can add a new category for your convinience by clicking on the add category button.
  4. Soon more features will be added so keep updating your app!
